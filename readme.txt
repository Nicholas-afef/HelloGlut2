current controls:
WASD movement
mouse - camera angle (max 89 pitch, max 360 yaw)
q and e - rotate object (currently fixed about 1, 1, 1)
1 and 2 - widen/shorten perspective
3 and 4 - move nearplane
5 and 6 - move farplane
